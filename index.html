Playtest: Mouse in Slippers

Hey there.

Mouse in Slippers is a game that I have been working on, and I would like to make it as good as the limits of my skill will allow. I really appreciate you taking the time to try out the game. In case you do not already have a link to the playtest release, here you go.

You can find the general feedback section below. There is a also a detailed level by level breakdown. This breakdown includes video solutions for all the levels as well, which could be spoilers if you haven't played the game yet. You can fill in the feedback for levels that you enjoyed or the ones you thought felt out of place. Your feedback will be saved in your browser, so you can fill it in at your own pace.

This release is mostly meant to test out how the game mechanics and levels play out. The art and music will be undergoing polish. The levels that are presented however, are representative of their final versions. All feedback is appreciated.

I hope you enjoy your time with Mouse in Slippers, and I thank you for all your feedback.
